About CODEZZY

We believe every student deserves access to world-class computer science education — completely free, forever.

Our Company Story

CODEZZY was born in 2024 when a group of engineering graduates noticed a troubling gap: millions of Indian students had the drive to learn technology, but lacked affordable, structured resources in plain English. Expensive bootcamps and scattered YouTube videos left beginners overwhelmed and unsure where to start.

We set out to build something different — a single platform where a first-year B.Tech student in Jaipur and a self-taught developer in Chennai could follow the same clear learning path. Starting with Python and HTML tutorials, CODEZZY quickly grew into a comprehensive library covering programming, web development, cyber security, AI, networking, Linux, cloud computing, and domain hosting.

Today, CODEZZY serves over 10,000 learners across India and beyond. Every tutorial is written by educators and industry practitioners who remember what it felt like to be a beginner — confused by jargon, intimidated by error messages, and hungry for practical skills that lead to real careers.

Our Mission

To democratize technology education by providing free, high-quality, beginner-friendly tutorials that empower students, career switchers, and job seekers to build real-world computer science skills — regardless of their financial background or geographic location.

We remove every barrier between curiosity and competence. No paywalls, no hidden fees, no premium tiers locking away essential content. Our mission is rooted in the belief that access to knowledge should never depend on how much money you have.

Our Vision

A world where every student can code, secure, and deploy — where rural colleges have the same learning resources as metropolitan universities, and where the next generation of Indian engineers builds products that compete on a global stage.

We envision CODEZZY becoming the go-to learning companion for 100 million students across South Asia, offering localized content, interactive projects, career mentorship, and a thriving community of learners who support each other from first "Hello World" to first job offer.

How We Teach

Our Learning Methodology

A proven four-step approach that takes you from confused beginner to confident practitioner.

Step 1

Learn the Fundamentals

Start with core concepts explained in plain language. We break down complex topics like data structures, the OSI model, or neural networks into digestible lessons with visual aids and real-world analogies.

Step 2

Practice with Examples

Follow along with annotated code snippets and interactive exercises. Type the code yourself, break it intentionally, and learn to read error messages — the most underrated developer skill.

Step 3

Build Real Projects

Apply your knowledge to guided projects — a portfolio website, a Python automation script, a network scanner, or a machine learning classifier. Projects cement learning and give you something to show recruiters.

Step 4

Grow Your Career

Use our career guidance, interview prep, resume templates, and certification roadmaps to translate skills into internships and jobs. Learning doesn't end at the last tutorial — it launches your career.
